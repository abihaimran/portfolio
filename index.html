Past Forward
Storytelling,
Branding,
Design
Past Forward is a concept that derives from the recognition that navigating through relationships in life, especially platonic companionships, often involves encountering negative experiences that prove to be painful setbacks in individuals' personal growth, affecting their self-esteem, trust, and the personal narrative of the life they have lived. Building on this awareness, Past Forward presents a constructive approach designed to empower individuals in overcoming the lingering bitterness from such negative experiences. This approach centres around revisiting and cherishing positive memories, offering a practical framework for healing and personal growth. This concept comes to life in the form of a book, consisting of 3 chapters; each a narrative from an individual about their journey moving on from such a relationship serving as an inspiration for the reader to begin their own journey of moving by harnessing the healing power of positive memories.
